On the Subject of Center Cube

Center...what? Where is the center?!

The module consists of a cube and 2 rings covering the centers of the cube. Only the rings are colored. To solve the module, figure out each side's center's color based on the rings colors (check R1NGS for more info).

Each side of each ring will contain one of the following colors: Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, Cyan, and Magenta. Each intersection will additively color mix the 2 ring colors and put it in where it intersects, but you can't see the mixed color. However, each center of the cube has a unique color.

Figure out what each center is, and press each one in a certain order. The order is based on the characters of the serial number.

  1. Take red, find the lowest character in the serial number using base-36, and place red under that character.
  2. If there is ever a tie between 2 characters, put the color under the leftmost character.
  3. Repeat until all 6 colors are under the serial number.

After you have obtained your color order, press the centers of the cube in that order to solve the module. If any center is pressed out of order, then a strike will be issued and the rings will change color.
